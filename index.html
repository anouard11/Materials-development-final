PEDAGOGY TARGETS
LEARNING OBJECTIVES

By the end of the unit, students will be able to:

  • ✓Ask and answer questions about household chores.
  • ✓Understand spoken information about home and daily life.
  • ✓Use vocabulary related to rooms in a house.
  • ✓Participate in information-gap speaking activities with classmates.
  • ✓Predict and understand short texts about a home using pictures and contextual clues.
